Uninstall Instructions

We're sorry to see you go! Clean Genie Pro follows standard Windows uninstallation procedures and leaves no trace behind.

Standard Removal (Windows 10 & 11)

1
Open Settings Click the Start Menu and select the Settings (Gear) icon, or press Windows Key + I.
2
Go to Apps Click on Apps > Installed Apps (or "Apps & Features").
3
Find Clean Genie Pro Type "Clean Genie Pro" in the search bar or scroll down the list to find it.
4
Uninstall Click the three dots (Windows 11) or click the app name (Windows 10) and select Uninstall. Follow the prompts to finish.

Need Help?
