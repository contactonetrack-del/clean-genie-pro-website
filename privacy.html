Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

At One Track, we believe that your system data belongs to you. Clean Genie Pro is built with a "Privacy First" architecture, meaning we do not collect, store, or sell your personal information.

1. No Personal Data Collection

Clean Genie Pro operates locally on your device. When you run a scan, the application analyzes files solely on your hard drive. No usage data, file contents, or personal identifiers are uploaded to our servers.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not track your browsing history.
  • We do not upload your files.
  • We do not sell any user data to third-party advertisers.

3. Local Processing

All optimization processing happens on your machine. The "Junk File" and "Privacy Trace" removal functions simply use standard Windows deletion commands initiated by you.

4. Application Analytics

We may collect anonymous, aggregate telemetry (e.g., "Installation Successful", "Crash Report") solely to improve application stability. This data is stripped of all personal identifiers.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at support@onetrack.com.
